Privacy Policy

Effective July 18, 2026 · Applies to the Walter app for Android (package app.walter.vault)

The short version: everything you put into Walter stays on your phone. Walter has no accounts, no servers, no analytics and no ads. We do not collect, transmit, sell or share any personal data, because the app never sends your data anywhere.

What the app stores, and where

Walter keeps the following information in local storage on your device only:

  • Your display name, wage or salary figure, and preferences you set in Settings
  • The impulses you lock up: item names, prices, categories, reasons and timers
  • Your savings totals, levels and achievements, which are computed from the above
  • A profile photo, if you choose one from your gallery. The image is copied into the app's private storage and never leaves your device

None of this is uploaded. If you are offline, Walter works exactly the same.

What we do not do

  • No user accounts and no sign-in
  • No analytics, tracking or advertising SDKs
  • No sharing or selling of data to third parties
  • No network transmission of your personal data

Permissions the app uses

  • Notifications: to remind you locally when a vault timer opens. Reminders are scheduled on your device.
  • Vibration: for haptic feedback on taps.
  • Photo library (optional): only if you choose to set a profile photo. Walter reads the single image you pick and nothing else.

Your data, your controls

  • Export: you can export your full history as a CSV file from the Profile screen. The file is saved where you choose; nothing is sent to us.
  • Delete: "Erase local data" in the app permanently removes your profile, preferences and vault history from the device. Uninstalling the app does the same.

Children

Walter is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from anyone, children included, because it does not collect information at all.

Changes to this policy

If a future version of Walter ever changes how data is handled, for example an optional backup feature, this policy will be updated first and the change will be called out in the app update notes.
